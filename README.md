# Waterloop "Gosling" Hyperloop Pod — Electrical

As **Electrical Team Lead** at Waterloop (University of Waterloo's Hyperloop team), I led the electrical design for the 6th-generation pod prototype "Gosling" — the levitating pod, motor control unit, and exterior lighting — and raised $5,000 in sponsorship by pitching the team to the UWaterloo Finance Association and local groups.
